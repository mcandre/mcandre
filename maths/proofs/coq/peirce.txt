Peirce's Law: ((p -> q) -> p) -> p

(p -> q) -> p    ASSUMPTION
~(p -> q) | p    ENTAILMENT

  [~(p -> q)]    ASSUMPTION
  ~(~p | q)    ENTAILMENT
  ~~p, ~q      DEMORGAN
  ~~p        AND-ELIMINATION
  p        DOUBLE NEGATION

  [p]        ASSUMPTION
  p        IDENTITY

p          EXHAUSTION

===================================
((p -> q) -> p) -> p
